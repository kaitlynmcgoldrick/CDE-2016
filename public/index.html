ABOUT

CDE is a conference for Engineering students across Canada to promote discussions on the topic of diversity in engineering. This year is the 2nd annual Conference for Diversity in Engineering and will be hosted by McGill University in the multicultural and bilingual city of Montreal.

We aim to bring together

200

delegates

coming from

10

provinces

to represent

51

engineering student societies.

THEME

This year's conference theme is

BUILDING MOMENTUM FOR CHANGE

During our time at the conference, we intend to build upon the current momentum of positive change that continues to make engineering an environment conducive for exploration and creativity.

ABOUT

Building Momentum for Change

Our Mission:

 Have every delegate understand and appreciate diversity as well as develop the tools, resources, and motivation to implement change in engineering.
